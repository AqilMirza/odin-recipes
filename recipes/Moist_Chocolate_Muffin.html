Lonestar1's Moist Chocolate Muffin

Description

A moist, delectable, sweet chocolate muffin.
Guaranteed to make anyone smile after taking a bite

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour

    • 2 cups of Flour

  • Sugar

    • 1 cup of white sugar

  • Cocoa Powder

    • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder

  • Chocolate Chips

    • 1 cup of chocolate chips

  • Baking Soda

    • A teaspoon of baking soda

  • Yogurt

    • 1 Cup of plain Yogurt

  • Milk

    • 1/2 cup of milk

  • Vegetable Oil

    • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil

  • Egg

    • 1 Egg

  • Vanilla Extract

    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. First

    2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
    Line 12 muffin cups with paper muffin liners.

  2. Second

    3. Combine flour, sugar, 3/4 cup chocolate chips,
    cocoa powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.

  3. Third

    4. Whisk yogurt, milk, oil, egg, and vanilla in separate bowl until smooth.
    Pour yogurt mixture into the chocolate mixture and stir until batter is just blended.

  4. Fourth

    5. Fill prepared muffin cups 3/4 full and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup chocolate chips.

  5. Fifth

    6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes.

  6. Sixth

    7. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
